# Isochrone Map -- Demo on a Neighborhood Near Shanghai High School

An **demo of isochrone map** for Junior Project--**Quality of Life in Shanghai**--that helps residents to explore the neighborhood easily.  

It visualizes nearby facilities within 5, 10, and 15 minutes of walking, helping users understand how accessible daily services are in their immediate environment.

## How to Access?

Open the link in broswer: https://alinaaaw.github.io/map/

## How to Use?

This demo is based on a **specific neighborhood** near Shanghai High School, which serves as the starting point.  
  
Users can use the buttons on the top-right corner to explore different **walking distances** and **facilities**, currently including:  
* Drinks  
* Bus stations  
* Hospitals

## Behind the Scene

### Project Motive

In Shanghai, rapid urban development contrasts with the presence of many **older neighborhoods**. These areas often face challenges such as safety concerns, management issues, and aging infrastructure, which may impact residents’ well-being.  

This project focuses on improving conditions in these neighborhoods, with the goal of enhancing residents’ **quality of life and overall happiness**.

### Statistics
A preliminary survey was conducted with a sample size of **48 participants** to support early-stage research.  

Key findings include:  

* 68.72% of residents usually choose to **walk** in their daily lives  
* 46.51% find software systems and apps **time-consuming to learn**  
* 32.56% believe apps have **too many features**, making them difficult to use  

The data presented above are **selectively included** to support the development of this website only; additional datasets were collected and utilized for other components of the overall project.  

These insights help inform design decisions and highlight the importance of **simplicity**, **accessibility**, and **practical** value in the project.
